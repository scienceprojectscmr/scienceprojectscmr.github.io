F.A.Q

  • Who can register for the events?

    The STEM bootcamp is for students between the ages of 13-16 inclusive only. For the event on women in computer science, preference will be shown for women/girls, but other students are free to participate if free slots are available. Any student can register for all the other events.

  • Do I need to pay to attend the events?

    Registration and attendance is free for all events.

  • Will there be coffee and lunch breaks?

    There will be coffee and lunch breaks for all events. All associated costs are handled by the organizers.

  • Do I need particular devices for the events?

    All kits for the STEM bootcamp will be provided by the organizers. Having a laptop is recommended for all other tutorials.

  • Do organizers cover transport or hotel expenses for participants?

    Participants need to cover their transport or hotel expenses. The organizers may provide transport assistance for few students with limited resources.

  • Could participants obtain a certificate of participation?

    All participants in the STEM bootcamp will be awarded a certificate of participation at the end of the event.
